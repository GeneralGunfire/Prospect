# Competitor & Market Research

**Project:** SA Career Guide
**Researched:** 2026-03-25 (live web verification)
**Confidence:** HIGH for competitor features (live-fetched), MEDIUM for market figures (official sources)

---

## Key Findings (Read First)

1. **Yenza has a free tier — but it's nearly useless.** R0 gets you a basic Worker Type assessment and career library browse. Anything meaningful (career matches, subject navigator, bursaries) costs R399–R999. Our product beats this by being 100% free with deeper features.

2. **Nobody has Grade 10–12 study content alongside career discovery.** Not Yenza, not Gradesmatch, not Khetha, not Careers Portal. Reslocate has an AI Tutor + past exam papers — that is the closest competitor on study content, but it's AI-generated (trust risk) and not curriculum-aligned subject explanations.

3. **No competitor has data-saver mode, WhatsApp sharing, or offline PWA.** These are genuinely uncontested features in the SA edtech space.

4. **TVET is underserved but Reslocate covers it.** Reslocate lists 54+ TVET colleges and supports TVET matching. Careers Portal also covers TVET opportunities. Our TVET advantage is TVET-as-career-pathway (NQF progression, what jobs it leads to) rather than just listing colleges.

5. **The addressable market is large.** ~705,000 Grade 12 candidates wrote matric in 2024. Add Grade 10 and 11 and the target market is ~2.1 million students annually. TVET adds another 482,000.

**Pricing:**
- Free: R0 — Worker Type assessment only, limited CV
- Lite: R399 one-time — Career matches, bursaries, free courses

**Our edge over Yenza:** 100% free, no login, Grade 10–12 study library, TVET pathways, data-saver mode, WhatsApp sharing, province map.

| Segment | Number | Source |

- **87.3%** matric pass rate in 2024 (historic high)
- **47.8%** qualified for bachelor degree entrance — highest ever
- Grade 9/10 subject choice decisions affect this pipeline 2–3 years earlier

### Mobile & Data Context

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| SA prepaid mobile users | 76% of phone users | GeoPoll |
| 1GB data cost (2023) | ~$1.18 / ≈R22 | Statista |
| Youth weekly data bundle example | 4GB + 4GB social for R49 | Vodacom 2025 |
| Smartphone penetration SA | High, 46.9M subscriptions | GeoPoll |

**Implication:** Data costs are real but falling. Our 50KB initial load target remains correct — students on prepaid weekly bundles cannot afford a 2MB page load multiplied across multiple visits. Zero-rating opportunities may exist (MTN, Vodacom educational zero-rating programmes worth investigating for distribution).

### NSFAS 2025 (Verified)

- **Household income threshold:** R350,000 per annum (confirmed 2025)
- **Loan scheme available:** R350,001–R600,000 (new from 2024)
- **Disabilities exemption:** Up to R600,000
- Our NSFAS checker should use R350K threshold for bursary eligibility, note the R350K–R600K loan zone separately.

## Go-to-Market Implications

### How Students Discover Tools
- **Teachers and guidance counsellors** are the primary referral channel for Yenza/Gradesmatch (school B2B)
- **WhatsApp groups** (class groups, family groups) are the primary peer-to-peer sharing mechanism
- **Google search** for "career guidance South Africa" / "what to study with my marks" is organic discovery
- **DBE / DHET communications** reach students via schools and SMS

### Distribution Angles (Not in Original Spec — New Finding)
1. **Zero-rating partnership:** MTN and Vodacom have educational zero-rating programmes. A zero-rated URL removes the data cost barrier entirely. Worth pursuing post-launch.
2. **WhatsApp share as growth loop:** If parent-shareable results are truly WhatsApp-compatible, each shared result reaches a new student. Design the share URL for virality (show career name + APS score in the preview).
3. **Careers Portal SEO moat:** Careers Portal dominates SA career-related search terms. Consider submitting to their partner programme rather than competing for the same search terms. Our differentiation is the study library + quiz, not the opportunity listings.
4. **Teacher shareability:** A "send to class" link for guidance counsellors to share with Grade 10 students at subject choice time is a high-leverage feature. Add to v2 roadmap.

### Competitive Positioning
- **vs Yenza:** "Same career guidance, fully free, with study content they don't have"
- **vs Gradesmatch:** "Career discovery first, not application management — find your path before you apply"
- **vs Reslocate:** "Curated study content you can trust, not AI guessing your matric curriculum"
- **vs Khetha/NCAP:** "Modern, fast, mobile-first version of the government tool — with a study library"
- **vs Careers Portal:** "Career discovery + study content, not just opportunity listings"
