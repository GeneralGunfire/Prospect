## Why These 150?

### Research Basis:
1. **Labour Market Gaps**: Identified skills shortages from Stats SA and SETA reports
2. **Job Postings**: LinkedIn, Indeed, PNet job posting trends 2024-2025
3. **Salary Trends**: Earning potential correlates with demand (higher = more in demand)
4. **Education Input**: Graduate absorption rates from universities and TVET colleges
5. **Industry Growth**: GDP growth by sector indicates opportunity
6. **Government Priority**: Skills development initiatives and funding priorities
7. **Emerging Tech**: Solar, renewable energy, data science represent future demand

### Excluded From Top 150:
- **Very niche careers** (e.g., Archaeologist, Astronomer) - low job volume
- **Declining careers** (e.g., Typist, Clerk) - automation reducing demand
- **Oversaturated roles** (e.g., General Bachelor of Arts, Graphic Designer - saturated in SA)
- **Very low salary** (e.g., Janitor) - though in demand, not aspiration-building
- **Fictional/rare careers** - for practical relevance
