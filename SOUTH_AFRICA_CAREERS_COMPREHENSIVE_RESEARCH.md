- Entry Level (1-3 yrs): R277,310/year

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR CAREER GUIDANCE

### For High School Students

**If You're STEM-Focused (Math, Science):**
→ Consider: AI Engineer, Software Engineer, Data Scientist, Medical Doctor, Civil Engineer, Pharmacist
→ These have highest salaries AND highest demand
→ Aim for 70%+ in Math and Physical Sciences

**If You're Non-STEM:**
→ Consider: Lawyer, Chartered Accountant, Psychologist, Business Management
→ Law requires NO specific subjects (just English 50%+)
→ Business requires Math but not strictly

**If You Want High Salary WITHOUT University:**
→ Consider: Welder (coded), Electrician (master), Plumber (own business), Carpenter
→ 2-4 years at TVET college
→ Entry salary competitive (R8k-R12k), senior can reach R25k-R30k/month
→ Self-employment potential crucial (can earn R25k-R50k+/month)

**If You Want Security + Growth:**
→ Consider: Nursing, Teaching, Engineering
→ High demand, stable government salaries, pension benefits
→ Growth into specializations and management

### Subjects to Focus On
- **Mathematics:** Required for engineering, computer science, accounting → Opens highest-paying doors
- **Physical Sciences:** Essential for engineering, medical, tech fields
- **Life Sciences:** Essential for medicine, nursing, pharmacy, psychology
- **English:** Required for ALL university entrance, especially law
- **Technical Drawing/Design:** Helps for engineering, architecture, design fields

### Emerging High-Demand Fields
1. **AI/Machine Learning** - Newest, highest growth
2. **Renewable Energy Engineering** - Solar, wind installation
3. **Cybersecurity** - Exponential threat growth
4. **Data Science** - Every industry needs data insights
5. **Coding/Software Development** - Permanent shortage

### Self-Employment Potential
- **Highest:** Plumbing, Carpentry, Welding, Hairdressing, Catering
- **Medium:** Engineering (consulting), Accounting (own practice), Psychology (private practice)
- **Lower:** Nursing (regulated), Teaching (limited), Medical doctor (regulated)
